Turn off electronics and lights when not in use

Electronics and lights continue to use electricity even when they are not in use. Make it a habit to turn off lights and unplug electronics when you're not using them to save energy and reduce your electricity bills.

Use renewable energy sources

Consider investing in solar panels or wind turbines to generate your own renewable energy. You can also switch to a utility company that uses renewable energy sources, such as wind or solar power, to power your home. This can help reduce your reliance on non-renewable sources of energy and promote sustainable energy practices.

Carry a reusable water bottle and Use water fountains

Instead of buying single-use plastic water bottles, carry a reusable water bottle with you. This can help reduce the amount of plastic waste generated and save you money in the long run. You could also make use of water fountains to refill your bottles or drink without having to buy a plastic bottled water. Luckily many public spaces such as airports, schools, and parks have water fountains available for public use..
